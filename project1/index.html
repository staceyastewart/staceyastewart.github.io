Welcome to Bunco! This is a dice game with 6 rounds. In each round, you are trying to roll the number of the round on the dice. For exameple, in round 1, you are aiming to roll 1's. If you roll three of a kind of the round number you get 21 points! If you roll three of a kind of another number, you get 5 points. Click "Roll Your Dice" to get started!
