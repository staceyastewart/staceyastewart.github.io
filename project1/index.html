The Game of Bunco!

Round 1

Welcome to Bunco! This is a dice game with 6 rounds. In each round, you are trying to roll the number of the round. For example, in round 1, you are aiming to roll 1's. If you roll three of a kind of the round number you get 21 points! If you roll three of a kind of another number, you get 5 points. Click "Roll Your Dice" to get started!
